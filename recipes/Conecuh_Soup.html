Conecuh Soup

DiscriptionThis Conecuh soup, made with Conecuh smoked pork sausage, is a perfect food that I bring to gatherings. It's great with cornbread, and feel free to dress it with cheese and sour cream. Final note, it is brand specific. I tried not using Bush’s black eyed peas, and it didn’t work—also my grandmother will turn over in her grave if you use a knockoff.

ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package Conecuh® pork sausage, any flavor, cut into 1-inch bites
  • 1 (10-ounce) can Ro*Tel® diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, undrained
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet Lipton® beefy onion soup
  • 4 (15.8 ounce) cans Bush's® black eyed peas, undrained
Steps
  1. Combine sausage, diced tomatoes and green chiles, beefy onion soup mix, and black eyed peas in a Dutch oven or a slow cooker
  2. Set Dutch oven over low heat on the stove (slow cooker set to Low), and cook until fully heated through, about 30 minutes (4 hours in the slow cooker)
